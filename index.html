Youth WorkTracker

Helping youth programmes manage remote internships and track progress

Problem

Youth internship programmes often place interns in different departments, but supervisors struggle to track tasks, monitor progress, and provide structured feedback. Communication happens across emails, messages, and spreadsheets, making it difficult to see how interns are performing.

Without a simple tracking system, programmes risk missed deadlines, limited accountability, and lost opportunities for mentorship.

Solution

Youth WorkTracker provides a simple digital space where supervisors assign tasks, track progress, and rate intern performance while interns can see their deadlines, progress, and feedback in one place.

Task Tracking

Supervisors assign tasks and deadlines to interns.

Progress Monitoring

Interns track their work completion using progress bars.

Private Feedback

Supervisors rate and review intern performance.

Department Oversight

Filter tasks by department for easier programme management.
